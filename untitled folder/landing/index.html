What you get

Built for real-world SEO workflows.

On-page + schema lint

Title, meta, canonicals, robots, headings, Open Graph, Twitter, JSON-LD and microdata with required-field checks and warnings.

Core Web Vitals snapshot

TTFB (net), FCP, LCP, CLS, INP, FID pulled directly from the page performance entries—no lab proxies.

Internal link intelligence

Header, on-page, and footer links with anchor text; click to jump and highlight on the live page.

SERP gap analysis

Change tracking
